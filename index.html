2. Location Data

ElBike requests location permission to track active rides and show nearby cycling tracks. Location is accessed only while the app is in use during a ride session. Background location is used solely to continue recording your route if you switch apps mid-ride — it is never used for advertising or tracking outside of rides.

GPS coordinates are stored locally on your device. Route data submitted to the leaderboard (speed and distance only — not full GPS traces) is stored on our servers. You can delete your ride history from within the app at any time.

3. Third-Party Services

ElBike integrates with the following third-party services:

  • 🗄️
    Supabase
    Stores your account, profile, ride leaderboard entries, reviews, and group rides. Data is hosted on servers within the EU. Privacy policy →
  • 🌤️
    Open-Meteo
    Provides weather forecasts. We send only your approximate GPS coordinates (latitude & longitude) to retrieve local weather. No personal data is shared. Terms →
  • ⛰️
    Open-Elevation
    Provides elevation data for route profiles. We send GPS coordinates only. No personal data is shared.
  • 🚴
    Strava (optional)
    If you choose to connect your Strava account, we request OAuth permission to upload your rides. We store only the access token (securely on your device). We do not read your full Strava activity history beyond what you explicitly choose to import. Privacy policy →

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide core app features: ride tracking, weather scores, route maps
  • Show your personal ride history, streaks, and analytics
  • Display community leaderboards and track reviews
  • Send local push notifications for weather reminders (only if you grant permission)
  • Sync rides to Strava (only when you explicitly tap "Upload to Strava")

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app features described above.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

On-device data (ride sessions, app settings) is stored indefinitely until you delete individual rides or uninstall the app.

Account data (profile, reviews, leaderboard entries, group rides) is retained while your account is active. You can delete your account by contacting us at the address below — all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Strava tokens are deleted from your device when you disconnect Strava in the app settings.

7. Children's Privacy

ElBike is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of ElBike after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For significant changes we will notify users via an in-app message.
